Alindahaw Beach Resort

Tukuran, Zamboanga del Sur

About

Alindahaw Beach Resort in Tukuran, Zamboanga del Sur is a hidden coastal gem perfect for a relaxing escape. Just a short drive from Pagadian City, it offers serene beaches, calm waters, and a laid-back vibe—ideal for unwinding and making memories by the shore.

Entrance and Cottage Rates

Entrance Fee: No entrance fee

Cottages:

  • ₱300 for up to 10 persons
  • ₱500 for up to 20 persons

Additional Fees: No extra or hidden fees

Operating Hours

Open: 6:00 AM
Close: 10:00 PM

Facilities and Amenities

  • Restrooms
  • Parking area
  • Grilling/kitchen area
  • Piso WiFi Vendo Machine
  • Trash bins
  • Salbabida
  • Overnight stays (Note: Swimming is not allowed at night)
  • 5 available rooms for overnight stay
  • Mini store for snacks, drinks, and essentials
  • Videoke machine
  • Designated event area
  • Small beachfront security hut
  • GCash cash-in and cash-out
  • Air-conditioned rooms
  • Emergency hotline tarpaulins

Overnight Stay Policy

Overnight stay is allowed, but swimming during night time is strictly prohibited.

Rules and Safety

Specific Rule: No swimming at night

Safety Personnel: Lifeguard and security personnel on duty

Additional Rules: Pets allowed, no dress code required

Visitor Information

Most Common Visitors: Local residents, especially from Zamboanga del Sur

Most Active Months: November to January
